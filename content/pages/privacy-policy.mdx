## Introduction

YouTube Automation AI ("we", "our", "the Service") helps creators and teams turn long-form videos into short-form assets. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

## What We Collect

1. **Account data**
   - Name, email, authentication identifiers, and profile information.
2. **Usage data**
   - Actions in the product (for example: waitlist submissions, page views, and feature usage).
3. **Technical data**
   - Device/browser type, IP-related metadata, and diagnostic logs.
4. **Payment data (if applicable)**
   - Billing metadata and transaction references from payment providers.
5. **Cookie/local storage data**
   - Session state, preferences, and analytics signals.

## How We Use Data

- Provide and operate the Service
- Improve product quality and performance
- Respond to support or security issues
- Process payments and prevent abuse/fraud
- Send product updates or onboarding notices

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data with:
- Infrastructure and hosting providers
- Authentication/payment providers
- Analytics and error monitoring tools
- Legal authorities when required by law

## Retention and Security

We retain data only as long as needed for operations, legal obligations, and security. We use industry-standard controls (access restrictions, encrypted transport, monitoring), but no system is risk-free.

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, deletion, or export of your data. Contact us to submit a request.

We may update this page over time. Material updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
