## Build an Automated YouTube Channel in 5 Steps

### 1. Pick one monetizable niche

Choose a niche with enough topic depth and realistic CPM potential.

### 2. Standardize script structure

Use a reusable narrative template: hook, context, steps, summary, CTA.

### 3. Build a no-face visual pipeline

Combine stock footage, B-roll, screen recordings, captions, and clean pacing.

### 4. Package for click and retention

Create strong thumbnails, clear value titles, and opening hooks that match viewer intent.

### 5. Run weekly performance reviews

Track CTR, retention, and watch time. Keep what works and cut what does not.
