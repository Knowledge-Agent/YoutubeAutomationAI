## How to Start YouTube Automation With AI (Action Plan)

### Week 1: Channel setup and niche validation

- Pick one audience promise.
- Build a 20-topic list.
- Draft title patterns and hook templates.

### Week 2: Build your production template

- Script template with hook, value, CTA.
- Editing template with timing and captions.
- Thumbnail template with one clear visual style.

### Week 3: Publish your first batch

- Publish 3-5 videos.
- Track CTR, retention, and average view duration.
- Collect drop-off points for iteration.

### Week 4: Optimization loop

- Keep winners, cut weak formats.
- Improve title + opening hook pairings.
- Plan next 4-week batch from data.
