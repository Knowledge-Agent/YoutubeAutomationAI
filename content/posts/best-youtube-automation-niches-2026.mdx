Niche choice is the highest-leverage decision in YouTube Automation. A strong workflow cannot fully compensate for a weak niche.

## CPM basics

CPM is revenue per 1,000 monetized views. Higher-CPM niches generally monetize faster with the same audience size.

### Typical CPM bands

- Finance / Business: **$15–50**
- Legal: **$12–30**
- Health: **$8–15**
- Tech: **$6–12**
- Entertainment: **$3–8**

## Top niches to evaluate

### 1. Finance and investing

- **CPM:** $15–50
- **Strengths:** advertiser value, evergreen demand, loyal viewers
- **Risks:** compliance accuracy and heavy competition

### 2. Business and entrepreneurship

- **CPM:** $12–25
- **Strengths:** strong commercial intent and broad subtopics
- **Risks:** credibility requirements and topic saturation

### 3. AI and productivity tech

- **CPM:** $6–12 (fast-rising)
- **Strengths:** strong trend tailwind and frequent content updates
- **Risks:** rapid obsolescence and keyword competition

### 4. History / documentary

- **CPM:** $5–12
- **Strengths:** evergreen topics and large source material base
- **Risks:** high research burden and storytelling quality demands

### 5. Health and fitness

- **CPM:** $8–15
- **Strengths:** stable audience demand and high engagement
- **Risks:** trust sensitivity and medical accuracy expectations

## Niche evaluation framework

Before committing, score each niche across:

1. Search demand trend
2. Competition intensity
3. Content source availability
4. Monetization depth (ads + affiliates + products)
5. Team capability and long-term interest

## 2026 emerging opportunities

- AI productivity workflows
- Sustainability / green lifestyle
- Remote work systems
- Mental performance and focus systems

## Selection checklist

### Must-have

- Proven demand
- Sustainable content pipeline
- Viable CPM and monetization

### Nice-to-have

- Multiple subniches
- Evergreen potential
- Lower creator concentration

### Red flags

- Highly saturated angle with no differentiation
- Legal/compliance exposure you cannot handle
- Niche requiring expertise you do not possess

Choose a niche you can publish in weekly for 12 months—consistency compounds faster than perfect positioning.
